About Us

Come to the first edition of our "Disney Festival" come retrace the stories of your greatest Disney classic of your childhood.
Between music, characters and retroprojections, come immerse yourself in the fantastic universe and let yourself be carried by the magic of Disney.
Great performers will come out to sing their songs, such as Panic at the Disco in "Into the Unknown" from the movie Frozen 2.
To celebrate Halloween, we will start the festival on the theme of the villains of the Disney universe
During the festival you will find the tributes of Walt disney and Annie Cordy between each song come to have your autograph books signed and take a picture with all the characters.
